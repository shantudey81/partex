About Us



Partex Plastics Limited


(PPL) began its commercial production in the year 2004 with an objective to produce high quality plastic products for both industrial and home use. These products include: Pipes, Furniture and Storage Boxes.

PPL has now become one of the largest producers of plastic products in Bangladesh. and are available to buy all over Bangladesh.

PPL mission is to become an internationally recognized manufacturer of plastic products.

Our employees are guided by the needs of our customers for exceptional service, quality products and value. We continuously strive to improve the skills, systems, service and products necessary to meet or exceed our customer's expectations.

Growing in an ever-changing marketplace is fundamental to our future. Responding to changes required by our valued old customers and important new customers will be accomplished through expansion of our facilities, broadening our market share and acquisition, as appropriate.

Our facilities perform a wide range of processes that include but are not limited to thermoset compression molding, both thermoset and thermoplastic injection molding, insert molding, thermoset injection/compression molding (I/C process), transfer molding, post-baking as needed, both manual & automated in-line & tumble deflashing methods, fine-grinding and lapping of critical sealing components, clean room inspections, robotics, and automated vision inspection systems.

PPL also recognizes the importance of being socially responsible. Our Environmental, Health, Safety & Security (EHS&S) Policy describes our dedication to safe, efficient operations that minimize negative impacts. For this reason we consistently seek sources of recyclable materials for use in making our products.

Simply put, Six Sigma is the way PPL works. The highly disciplined Six Sigma process helps us focus on developing and delivering near-perfect products and services. It encompasses design, production, and distribution.

With access to some of the world's leading experts in polymer technology and applications, our market-driven innovations have positioned the company for sustainable future growth. PPL is continuously seeking ecologically compatible applications for existing products, and improvements in the energy efficiency of our own production processes. PPL is dedicated to operational excellence and strive to provide the highest level of customer service and satisfaction with our materials.

We recognize that a strong profit base must be maintained with a human resource program that continues to recruit and develop outstanding people.

We will continue to develop our business with the energy and pride that has made us strong since our beginning.
